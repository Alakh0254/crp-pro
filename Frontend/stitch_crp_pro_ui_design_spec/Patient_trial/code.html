Type 2 Diabetes — Blood Sugar Study

Condition: Type 2 Diabetes

Open
location_on City General Hospital, Pune schedule About 12 weeks

About this study

This study is testing a new once-daily tablet designed to help the body manage blood sugar more effectively. Over about 12 weeks you’ll have regular check-ups with the study team, who will monitor your health throughout. The aim is to learn whether the tablet improves blood sugar control compared with current treatments.

Who can take part

  • check_circle Adults aged 18–70
  • check_circle Diagnosed with Type 2 Diabetes for at least 6 months
  • check_circle Not currently pregnant or breastfeeding

Not sure if you qualify? You can still apply — the study team will confirm your eligibility.
